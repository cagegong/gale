7
He's `coming in for` a meeting with Anne.
<k>come in for sth=来这里为某事

I only come in for the meetings.

I only come in when you need to fix your computers.

注意这里in和for两个词连用, 你可能觉得奇怪, 因为in是副词, 后边不需要接任何东西

好比我们说请进come in.
超车 cut in</k>

9
`What this organization needs is` talent.
<k>organize=v 组织
organization=n 组织机构
organizer=n 组织者

What this organizaiton needs is...
我们从这个句子中可以提炼出如下用法
What sb needs is

What I need is my business cards.
What I need is some understanding.
注意既可以是具体的东西, 也可以是抽象的东西.

推而广之得到What I want/need/like is
What I want is some coffee.
What I like is some quietness.
</k>

talent=才干, 才能
I have a talent for music.
Anne has a talent for remembering things.
</k>

But `we'd prefer` someone presentable,with a pleasant manner.
<k>we'd prefer=we would prefer

Young people would prefer low prices to large portions.</k>

11
But we'd prefer `someone presentable`,with a pleasant manner.
<k>presentable=可以present给别人看的, 看起来还不错的
good looking=长得好看的
注意区别二者.

someone presentable=的人, 就是长得还拿得出手.
我们从中学到了someone+adj的表达

Someone tall.
someone talented.</k>

11
But we'd prefer someone presentable,`with a pleasant manner`.
<k>pleasant=adj 令人愉快的
manner=n 行为 举止 [SINGULAR]
a way of behaving or speaking

with a pleasant manner=有令人愉快的举止

with a lovely view of the park
with long blonde hair</k>

yet也可以后边跟其他结构来表示专业.

He's 26, yet he can't cook(跟一个句子)</k>
