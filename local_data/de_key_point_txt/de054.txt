Hm. Do you know her schedule `by any chance`?
<k>by any chance
碰巧, 用于疑问句
chance是机会, 机会都是偶然碰到的, 而不是注定会有的, by any chance就是碰巧.
这里的any不能丢掉

Do you remember our CEO's name, by any chance?
Do you know Anne's birthday, by any chance?

by chance
碰巧, 用户肯定句和否定句
I met Tom by chance yesterday.
昨天我偶然遇见汤姆。
I won purely by chance.
我完全是侥幸赢的。
</k>
